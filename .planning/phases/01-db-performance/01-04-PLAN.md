What "single connection" means in Diesel async:
- One call to `self.db.connect().await?` → one pool checkout
- All subsequent `.load::<T>(&mut connection).await` calls reuse that single checkout
- Correct pattern: three `.load::<T>(&mut connection).await` calls, one `self.db.connect().await?`
- Incorrect pattern: `self.db.connect().await?` called inside a loop or repeated per sub-query
